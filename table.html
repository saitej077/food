Making of TEA

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add tea leaves and sugar
  3. Let it simmer for a few minutes
  4. Strain the tea into a cup and milk if desired.

Making of Aloo Chapathi

  1. kneed the dough for paratha Let it rest
  2. For aloo curry,saute onions,tomatoes,and spices in a pan
  3. Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickens

Making of Biryani

  1. Marinate the meal and vegetableswith spices and yogart
  2. Cook the rice until it's 70% done
  3. Layer the marinated meat vegetables and rice in a pot
  4. Cook on low heat(dum) until fully cooked.
